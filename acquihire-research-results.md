## Summary

Total unique cases analyzed: 20

### Key Legal Frameworks Identified

1. **Revlon Duties** - Enhanced scrutiny when board approves sale of control
2. **Entire Fairness Standard** - Applied when controlling shareholder on both sides
3. **Fiduciary Duty of Loyalty** - Directors must act in shareholders' best interest
4. **Minority Shareholder Protection** - Delaware courts scrutinize squeeze-outs
5. **Zone of Insolvency** - Expanded duties when company nears insolvency

### Relevant Legal Theories for "Acqui-Hire" Challenges

- **Breach of Fiduciary Duty**: Directors who approve deals benefiting themselves
- **Self-Dealing**: Management negotiating for personal benefits in acquisition
- **Fraudulent Transfer**: Assets conveyed to avoid creditor/shareholder claims
- **Option Holder Rights**: Claims based on plan documents and equity commitment
